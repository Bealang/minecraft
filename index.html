How to Use Minecraft Color Codes & Hex Colors

Welcome to our chat formatter! Enhancing your server's chat, signs, and MOTD is easy with Minecraft color codes. By simply using the ampersand (&) followed by a specific character (like a-f or 0-9), you can bring vibrant shades to your text.

Modern Minecraft also supports custom hex colors (e.g., &#FF5555), allowing you to pick millions of distinct color combinations. Use this tool to visualize and generate the exact combinations before putting them in-game.
